Short Bio

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Heng Ji. Before coming to UIUC, I got my bachelor degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I was a member of Zhiyuan's Honors Program of Engineering.

My research mainly lies in Information Extraction and Knowledge-enhanced Natural Language Processing. Recently, I am particularly interested in pre-training language models with self-supervised structure mining.
